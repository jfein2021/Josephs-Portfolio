About Me

My name is Joey and I am a finance graduate from the Fisher College of Business at the Ohio State University. I earned my Bachelor of Science in Business Administration with a Specialization in Finance and a minor in MME [Music, Media, and Enterprise]. I have a passion for business, finance, entrepreneurship, and leadership.

I strive to find roles where I can apply my passion for business and leadership. I recently interned at Shore Morgan Young Wealth Strategies, where I gained experience and learned about the world of financial planning. At OSU, I was the president for Ohio State's Rock Music Club, where I helped plan local concerts, events, and meetings.

I enjoy working in places where I can contribute and feel apart of the community, and I strive for a future where both my life and workplace can model around that.
